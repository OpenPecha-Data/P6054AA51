yaṃ caiva so bhāṣati lokanāthoekāsanasthaḥ pravarāgradharmam|taṃ sarvamādhārayi so jinātmajovaraprabho
yo abhu dharmabhāṇakaḥ

so co jino bhāṣiya agradharmaṃpraharṣayitvā janatāmanekām|tasmiṃśca divase vadate sa nāyakaḥpurato
hi lokasya sadevakasya

prakāśitā me iya dharmanetrīācakṣito dharmasvabhāva yādṛaśaḥ|nirvāṇakālo mama adya bhikṣavorātrīya
yāmasmiha madhyamasmin

bhavathāpramattā adhimuktisārāabhiyujyathā mahya imasmi śāsane|sudurlabhā bhonti jinā maharṣayaḥkalpāna
koṭīnayutāna atyayāt

saṃtāpajātā bahubuddhaputrāduḥkhena cogreṇa samarpitābhavan|śrutvāna ghoṣaṃ dvipadottamasyanirvāṇaśabdaṃ
atikṣiprametat

āśvāsayitvā ca narendrarājātāḥ prāṇakoṭyo bahavo acintiyāḥ|mā bhāyathā bhikṣava nirvṛte mayibhaviṣyatha
buddha mamottareṇa

śrīgarbha eṣo vidu bodhisattvogatiṃ gato jñāni anāsravasmin|spṛśiṣyate uttamamagrabodhiṃvimalāgranetro
ti jino bhaviṣyati

tāmeva rātriṃ tada yāmi madhyameparinirvṛto hetukṣaye va dīpaḥ|śarīra vaistāriku tasya cābhūtstūpāna
koṭīnayutā anantakā

bhikṣuśca tatrā tatha bhikṣuṇīyoye prasthitā uttamamagrabodhim|analpakāste yatha gaṅgabālikāabhiyukta
tasyo sugatasya śāsane

yaścāpi bhikṣustada dharmabhāṇakovaraprabho yena sa dharma dhāritaḥ|aśīti so antarakalpa pūrṇāṃtahi
śāsane bhāṣati agradharmān

aṣṭāśataṃ tasya abhūṣi śiṣyāḥparipācitā ye tada tena sarve|dṛṣṭā ca tebhirbahubuddhakoṭyaḥsatkāru
teṣāṃ ca kṛto maharṣiṇām

caryāṃ caritvā tada ānulomikīṃbuddhā abhūvan bahulokadhātuṣuparasparaṃ te ca anantareṇaanyonya
vyākarṣu tadāgrabodhaye

teṣāṃ ca buddhāna paraṃpareṇadīpaṃkaraḥ paścimako abhūṣi|devātidevo ṛṣisaṃghapūjitovinītavān
prāṇisahasrakoṭyaḥ

yaścāsi tasyo sugatātmajasyavaraprabhasyo tada dharma bhāṣataḥ|śiṣyaḥ kusīdaśca sa lolupātmālābhaṃ
ca jñānaṃ ca gaveṣamāṇaḥ

yaśorthikaścāpyatimātra āsītkulākulaṃ ca pratipannamāsīt|uddeśa svādhyāyu tathāsya sarvona tiṣṭhate
bhāṣitu tasmi kāle

nāmaṃ ca tasyo imamevamāsīdyaśakāmanāmnā diśatāsu viśrutaḥ|sa cāpi tenākuśalena karmaṇākalmāṣabhūtenabhisaṃskṛtena

ārāgayī buddhasahasrakoṭyaḥpūjāṃ ca teṣāṃ vipulāmakārṣīt|cīrṇā ca caryā vara ānulomikīdṛṣṭaśca
buddho ayu śākyasiṃhaḥ

ayaṃ ca so paścimako bhaviṣyatianuttarāṃ lapsyati cāgrabodhim|maitreyagotro bhagavān bhaviṣyativineṣyati
prāṇasahasrakoṭyaḥ

kausīdyaprāptastada yo babhūvaparinirvṛtasya sugatasya śāsane|tvameva so tādṛśako babhūvaahaṃ
ca āsīttada dharmabhāṇakaḥ

imena haṃ kāraṇahetunādyadṛṣṭvā nimittaṃ idamevarūpam|jñānasya tasya prathitaṃ nimittaṃprathamaṃ
mayā tatra vadāmi dṛṣṭam

dhruvaṃ jinendro'pi samantacakṣuḥśākyādhirājaḥ paramārthadarśī|tameva yaṃ icchati bhāṣaṇāyaparyāyamagraṃ
tadadyo mayā śrutaḥ

tadeva paripūrṇa nimittamadyaupāyakauśalya vināyakānām|saṃsthāpanaṃ kurvati śākyasiṃhobhāṣiṣyate
dharmasvabhāvamudrām

prayatā sucittā bhavathā kṛtāñjalībhāṣiṣyate lokahitānukampī |varṣiṣyate dharmamanantavarṣaṃtarpiṣyate
ye sthita bodhihetoḥ

yeṣāṃ ca saṃdehagatīha kācidye saṃśayā yā vicikitsa kācit|vyapaneṣyate tā vidurātmajānāṃye bodhisattvā
iha bodhiprasthitāḥ

ityāryasaddharmapuṇḍarīke dhamaparyāye nidānaparivarto nāma prathamaḥ
