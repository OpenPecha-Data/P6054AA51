caṃkramedvā, tatra ajita tathāgatamuddiśya caityaṃ kartavyam, tathāgatastūpo'yamiti ca
sa vaktavyaḥ sadevakena lokeneti||atha khalu bhagavāṃstasyāṃ velāyāmimā gāthā abhāṣata—puṇyaskandho
aparyanto varṇito me punaḥ punaḥ|ya idaṃ dhārayetsūtraṃ nirvṛte naranāyake

pūjāśca me kṛtāstena dhātustūpāśca kāritāḥ|ratnāmayā vicitrāśca darśanīyaḥ suśobhanāḥ

brahmalokasamā uccā chatrāvalibhiranvitāḥ|pariṇāhavantaḥ śrīmanto vaijayantīsamanvitāḥ

paṭughaṇṭā raṇantaśca paṭṭadāmopaśobhitāḥ|vāteritāstathā ghaṇṭā śobhanti jinadhātuṣu

pūjā ca vipulā teṣāṃ puṣpagandhavilepanaiḥ|kṛtā vādyaiśca vastraiśca dundubhībhiḥ punaḥ puna

madhurā vādyabhāṇḍā ca vāditā teṣu dhātuṣu|gandhatailapradīpāśca dattāste'pi samantataḥ

ya idaṃ dhārayet sūtraṃ kṣayakāli ca deśayet|īdṛśī me kṛtā tena vividhā pūjanantikā

agrā vihārakoṭyo'pi bahuścandanakāritāḥ|dvātriṃśatī ca prāsādā uccaistvenāṣṭavattalāḥ

śayyāsanairupastabdhāḥ khādyabhojyaiḥ samanvitāḥ|praveṇī praṇīta prajñaptā āvāsāśca sahasraśaḥ

ārāmāścaṃkramā dattāḥ puṣpārāmopaśobhitāḥ|bahu ucchadakāścaiva bahurūpavicitritāḥ

saṃghasya vividhā pūjā kṛtā me tena saṃmukham|ya idaṃ dhārayetsūtraṃ nirvṛtasmin vināyake

adhimuktisāro yo syādato bahutaraṃ hi saḥ|puṇyaṃ labheta yo etatsūtraṃ vācellikheta vā

likhāpayennaraḥ kaścit suniruktaṃ ca pustake|pustakaṃ pūjayettacca gandhamālyavilepanaiḥ

dīpaṃ ca dadyādyo nityaṃ gandhatailasya pūritam|jātyutpalātimuktaiśca prakaraiścampakasya ca

kuryādetādṛśīṃ pūjāṃ pustakeṣu ca yo naraḥ|bahu prasavate puṇyaṃ pramāṇaṃ yasya no bhavet

yathaivākāśadhātau hi pramāṇaṃ nopalabhyate||diśāsu daśasū nityaṃ puṇyaskandho'yamīdṛśaḥ

kaḥ punarvādo yaśca syāt kṣānto dāntaḥ samāhitaḥ|śīlavāṃścaiva dhyāyī ca pratisaṃlānagocaraḥ

akrodhano apiśunaścaityasmin gaurave sthitaḥ|bhikṣūṇāṃ praṇato nityaṃ nādhimānī na cālasaḥ

prajñavāṃścaiva dhīraśca praśnaṃ pṛṣṭo na kupyati|anulomaṃ ca deśeti kṛpābuddhī ca prāṇiṣu

ya īdṛśo bhavetkaścid yaḥ sūtraṃ dhārayedidam|na tasya puṇyaskandhasya pramāṇamupalabhyate

yadi kaścinnaraḥ paśyedīdṛśaṃ dharmabhāṇakam|dhārayantamidaṃ sūtraṃ kuryādvai tasya satkriyām

divyaiśca puṣpaistatha okiretadivyaiśca vastrairabhicchādayeta|mūrdhena vanditva ca tasya pādautathāgato'yaṃ
janayeta saṃjñām

dṛṣṭvā ca taṃ cintayi tasmi kālegamiṣyate eṣa drumasya mūlam|budhyiṣyate bodhimanuttarāṃ śivāṃhitāya
lokasya sadevakasya

yasmiṃśca so caṃkrami tādṛśo viduḥtiṣṭheta vā yatra niṣīdayedvā|śayyāṃ ca kalpeya kahiṃci dhīrobhāṣantu
gāthāṃ pi tu ekasūtrāt

yasmiṃśca stūpaṃ puruṣottamasyakārāpayeccitrasudarśanīyam|uddiśya buddhaṃ bhagavanta nāyakaṃpūjāṃ
ca citrāṃ tahi kārayettathā

mayā sa bhuktaḥ pṛthivīpradeśomayā svayaṃ caṃkramitaṃ ca tatraṃ|tatropaviṣṭo ahameva ca syāṃyatra
sthitaḥ so bhavi buddhaputraḥ

iti śrīsaddharmapuṇḍarīke dharmaparyāye puṇyaparyāyaparivarto nāma ṣoḍaśamaḥ||
